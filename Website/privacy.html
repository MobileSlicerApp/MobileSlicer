Privacy

Beta data note.

The beta form collects only what is needed to understand tester device and printer coverage.

Data collected

The beta form stores Android device, 3D printer, testing interests, and the time the registration was created. Email is not required.

Feedback data

The feedback form stores feedback type, title, details, submission time, and optional image attachments when provided.

How it is used

The information is used for beta development, fixing bugs, improving workflows, and prioritizing device and printer compatibility.

App telemetry

The website registration flow does not enable automatic app telemetry. If a future beta build adds diagnostics, that behavior should be described here before the build is distributed.

Retention and removal

Beta records are kept while they are useful for testing and compatibility work. Because the form does not collect email, records are not tied to a direct contact address.

Download security

After registration succeeds, the site creates a signed download link that expires after 20 minutes. The APK is served through a Cloudflare Function instead of a permanent public asset URL.
