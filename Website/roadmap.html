Roadmap

Focused beta first.

The project is prioritizing a reliable mobile slicer flow before expanding into deeper desktop-class tools.

Now

Beta workflow

STL import, touch workspace, profile context, on-device slicing, native preview, and APK feedback.

Next

Coverage and polish

More printer/profile coverage, cleaner export/share behavior, tester feedback review, and stronger tablet layouts.

Later

Advanced slicer controls

Broader process settings, richer 3MF handling, and advanced model operations where they make sense on mobile.
