About

A mobile-native slicer, not a desktop port.

MobileSlicer keeps proven slicer concepts underneath while reshaping the interface for touch, small screens, and quick device handoff.

Built for the Android workflow.

The project is a native Android app with a Kotlin and Compose interface, a dedicated model workspace, and a wrapped OrcaSlicer engine for the heavy slicing work.

The goal is direct: import a model, choose printer and material context, slice on-device, preview the output, then export without needing a desktop in the loop.
