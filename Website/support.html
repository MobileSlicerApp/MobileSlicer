MobileSlicer.

MobileSlicer is committed to always being free, open source, and ad free. I will never add features that ruin the user experience, charge for certain features, or use financial incentives to pressure anyone into supporting the project.

Support is completely optional. If you want to help development, every contribution goes directly into the project, including testing on more phones and tablets, buying development equipment, and making it possible to invest more time into MobileSlicer.
